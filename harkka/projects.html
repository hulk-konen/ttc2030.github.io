Factors Affecting Specialty Coffee Prices

Master's Thesis

Auctions provide an interesting view into price formation. In some senses laws of supply and demand are in their purest form in the auction marketplaces.

With various quality data, I analyzed 2105 green coffee lots sold through specialty coffee auctions. For example, growing altitude and variety were significant factors among over dozen other product attributes. Analysis was done as a regression model, with truncated maximum likelihood estimation.
